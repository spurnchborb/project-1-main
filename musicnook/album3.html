TWICE

Quick facts: TWICE is a 9-member South Korean girl group under JYP Entertainment. The group consists of Jihyo, Nayeon, Jeongyeon, Momo, Sana, Mina, Dahyun, Chaeyoung, and Tzuyu. The group was formed through the survival show SIXTEEN and debuted on October 20, 2015.
